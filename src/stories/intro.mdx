# MotionXY Introduction

The ideal IoT platform should be easily discoverable and user-friendly. It should have a well-designed user interface and provide comprehensive documentation and support. The platform should also offer a wide range of features and capabilities to meet the diverse needs of users.

`yarn/npm install`
